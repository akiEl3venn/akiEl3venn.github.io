关于我 About Me

我是一名热衷于挖掘数据背后故事的体验设计师。我相信好的设计不仅仅是视觉上的愉悦，更是基于严谨的研究与对人类行为的深刻理解。

在设计与技术的交叉点上，我致力于构建既有逻辑美感又充满人文关怀的数字产品。 I am an experienced designer passionate about uncovering the stories behind data. I believe good design is not just visual pleasure, but rooted in rigorous research and a deep understanding of human behavior.

At the intersection of design and technology, I strive to build digital products that possess both logical beauty and humanistic care.
